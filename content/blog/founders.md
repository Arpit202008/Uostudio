# Introduction of Founders - UOStudio

UOStudio is built on vision, creativity, and passion, led by its founders Arpit Chourasia and Raushan Raj. With a shared ambition to create immersive and engaging 2D experiences, they are steadily shaping UOStudio into a growing name in the game development industry.

## Founder – Arpit Chourasia

Arpit Chourasia is the founder of UOStudio, a passionate developer and entrepreneur dedicated to creating interactive digital experiences. With strong expertise in programming and game design, he drives the technical innovation and strategic direction of the studio.

## Co-Founder – Raushan Raj

Raushan Raj is the creative force behind UOStudio. With a keen eye for design and a deep passion for storytelling, he transforms ideas into visually engaging and immersive 2D worlds that define the studio’s unique identity.
